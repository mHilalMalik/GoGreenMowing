DESIGN DIRECTION:

Design a premium, editorial-style website for Go Green Mowing, a local eco-friendly lawn care company based in South Surrey, British Columbia, Canada. The aesthetic should feel like a high-end landscaping studio — mature, trustworthy, and clean. Reference: Terrain landscaping template. NO cartoon elements, NO bubbly UI, NO rounded pill buttons everywhere. The mascot (friendly green mower character) is used only once, small, in the footer or about section as a brand mark — never as a hero element.


TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM:

Headlines: Playfair Display — serif, italic for emphasis words, commanding and editorial. Body & UI: DM Sans — clean, modern, slightly warm. Labels: DM Sans 600 weight, 0.7rem, wide letter-spacing, uppercase, lime green color. Never use Inter, Roboto, or Arial.
